Magic Mavs

Genre: Augmented Reality Action/Adventure Game Developed: August 2024

Magic Mavs is an AR Action/Adventure Game set on the University of Texas at Arlington campus. The game follows a student who lives in a world where humans and mavericks, also known as blazes, coexist together. The student is tasked by the leaders of the Basement of the UTA Central Library to find hidden blazes in horse statues all over campus and befriend them to join the journey.
